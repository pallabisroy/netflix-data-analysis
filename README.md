# Netflix Data Exploration and Visualization

## Problem Statement
Analyze Netflix's catalog of 8,807 titles to identify content patterns and provide data-driven recommendations for content strategy and business growth.

## Dataset
- Records: 8,807
- Features: 12
- Source: Netflix catalog as of mid-2021

## Key Findings
- Movies dominate (69.6%) but TV Show share is growing rapidly
- United States leads content production (3,690 titles), India is second (1,046)
- TV-MA is the most common rating: family content is underrepresented at 13%
- Content additions peak in December-January and July
- Most TV shows are single-season

## Recommendations
1. Increase TV Show production toward a 50/50 split
2. Invest heavily in Indian and South Korean content
3. Expand family-friendly programming
4. Invest in multi-season TV shows to reduce churn
5. Leverage documentaries as a cost-effective content category
